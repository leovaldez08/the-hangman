The Hangman Quiz

Game Instructions

    1) A random word will be asked from the selected topic and the player should try to guess the answer.
    2) The player gets 6 chances to guess one answer. For each wrong guess the hangman will be hanged step by step.
    3) Once the number of wrong guesses reaches 6, the hangman will be hanged to death and the game will be over.
    4) Player can take hint from the number of underscores (_) present in the answer field.
    5) For each correct answer, the player will be rewarded with one score and there's no negative scores.
